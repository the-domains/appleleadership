My name is Scott Greer and I am a PhD student at Alliant International University. I am studying the concept of leadership and I am fascinated and intrigued by Apple. Because of this and my desire to tie my work to something that interests me, I have decided to look at different events and leadership decisions with Apple using academic models and concepts. 

 My belief is that Apple is a unique corporation that has the ability to change more than just the technology it creates, but the entire world of business by leading in its practices. Far too often, I have heard as I have traveled through school, "that's just how Apple does it. No one else can do it that way." I disagree. I believe by not studying this company closer, many corporations and organizations are not reaching the full potential their companies have to offer. If leaders of today's organizations can at least be open to the possibility that what Apple does is not only make great products, but also create a culture where innovation can thrive. I believe that innovation and excellence is what all effective leaders strive for within organizations.
